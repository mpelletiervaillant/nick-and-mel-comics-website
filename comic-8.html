Chick Flicks

August 18, 2020

Nick explains why Mel is always so lost while watching movies.

I like watching chick flicks... so when we watch Nick's movie pick I often ask a lot of questions really early on, like 'Wait who is that?', and he says 'I don't know, I'm watching the same movie as you are'. It took him a while to figure out that the reason I ask so many questions early on is because Chick Flicks essentially spoil the entire plot in the first 5 minutes of exposition...

-Mel
