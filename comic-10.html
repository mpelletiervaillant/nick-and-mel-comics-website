Harry Potter

August 28 2022

Nick convinces Mel to give Harry Potter another chance.

My parents had shown us Harry Potter when we were kids... but I got so scared by Voldemort that I refused to sleep (my mom finally resulted to putting my Power Puff Girl toys near my door promising they would protect me from him). After that traumatic experience, I never gave the franchise another chance. Finally Nick convinced me, and well, let's just say I get it now.

-Mel
